Mercury

Hi! I'M Mercury

  • The smallest planet in our solar system
  • 3 characteristics of Mercury:
    • Only the second hottest planet.
    • Has a molten core.
    • Most cratered planet in the Solar System.


Venus

Hi! I'M Venus

  • The second planet from the Sun.
  • 3 characteristics of Venus:
    • A day on Venus is longer than a year.
    • Hotter than Mercury despite being further away from the Sun.
    • Spins clockwise on its axis.


Earth

Hi! I'M Earth

  • Our home planet
  • 3 characteristics of Earth:
    • Has a solid and active surface with mountains, valleys, canyons, plains and so much more.
    • 70% of the Earth's Surface is Covered in Water.
    • Almost a Sphere.


Mars

Hi! I'M Mars

  • Known as the Red Planet
  • 3 characteristics of Mars:
    • Used to have a thicker atmosphere.
    • Smaller than Earth.
    • Has two moons.


Jupiter

Hi! I'M Jupiter

  • A gas giant planet
  • 3 characteristics of Jupiter:
    • Made up of mostly Hydrogen and Helium gas.
    • Covered in thick red, brown, yellow and white clouds.
    • Rotates or spins faster than any other planet


Saturn

Hi! I'M Saturn

  • The Ringed Planet.
  • 3 characteristics of Saturn:
    • Its beautiful rings are not solid.
    • Saturn could float in water because it is mostly made of gas.
    • It is very windy on Saturn.


Uranus

Hi! I'M Uranus

  • The seventh planet from the Sun
  • 3 characteristics of Uranus:
    • Made of water, methane, and ammonia fluids.
    • Uranus has rings.
    • Has 27 moons.


Neptune

Hi! I'M Neptune

  • Eighth and farthest-known Solar planet from the Sun
  • 3 characteristics of Mercury:
    • Spins on its axis very rapidly.
    • The smallest of the ice giants.
    • Has a very thin collection of rings.
